1. TO PRESERVE
The East-India Trade,

    _ It's Proposed

    _ To this probably, by the now present Company, it may be objected and said, That

    _ To this it is to be answered:

    _ The Landed-man before he well has considered the matter, and his real and true interest
in it, may also (it may be) object and say;

    _ In Answer to this may be said, That
